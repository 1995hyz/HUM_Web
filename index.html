The Cooper Union Fairy Tales

This website contains the original fairy tales produced by students of The Cooper Union as part of the course HUM-243-1: The Fairy Tale Fall 2019, taught by Professor Harold N. Ramdass

As part of this production, a physical book was created and can be found at The Cooper Union Library. Only a selection of the fairy tales produced in the course were included in the physical book. Here we present to you all the works ordered by the last name of the author.
